Your Academic Homepage: Outreach Booster Or Narcissistic Outlet?

Do you really need an academic homepage? Considering the abundance of academic homepages and blogs around, I’m certainly not the only one who asked himself that question. I’ve been playing with the idea of making one myself for a quite a while now, and finally decided to give it a shot. You’re currently looking at the result. For those of you interested in the technical details or the idea behind the domain name, there are posts in the making on that.
